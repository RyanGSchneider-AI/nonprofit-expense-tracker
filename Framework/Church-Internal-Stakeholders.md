## 2. Stakeholder Directory

### 2.1 Pastoral and Executive Leadership

| Role | Areas of Concern |
|---|---|
| **Pastor** | Overall congregational health, ministry program effectiveness, staff wellbeing, denominational relationships. Engaged in financial matters at the strategic level — budget direction, major expenditure decisions, apportionment commitments. Does not manage day-to-day finances. |
| **Lay Leader** | Lay representation to the district and conference; bridge between congregation and denominational structure. Participates in Church Council. |

### 2.2 Finance and Administration

| Role | Areas of Concern |
|---|---|
| **Treasurer** | Financial accuracy, chart of accounts integrity, financial reporting to Finance Committee and congregation, coordination with Office Administrator on expense tracking, QuickBooks management. Currently leading a chart of accounts cleanup and reporting improvement effort. Primary owner of the expense coordination application. |
| **Finance Committee Chair** | Financial oversight, budget compliance, reserve management, reporting to Church Council. First audience for Treasurer reports. |
| **Finance Committee Members** | Budget compliance, financial health of the church, accurate reporting. May review reports but do not enter transactions. |
| **Office Administrator** | Day-to-day administrative operations, expense processing, vendor payments, receipt management. Primary operational user of the expense coordination application. Needs to classify expenses correctly against chart of accounts without requiring deep accounting knowledge. |

### 2.3 Facilities

| Role | Areas of Concern |

| **Board of Trustees Chair** | Building and grounds condition, capital maintenance priorities, insurance. Coordinates with Finance Committee on capital expenditure funding. |
| **Trustees** | Specific maintenance and improvement projects, property stewardship. May generate expense requests that flow through the Office Administrator. |

## 3. Committees and Decision Authority

> Financial decision authority flows through the Finance Committee and Church Council.
> The table below clarifies who can authorize what.

| Decision Type | Who Initiates | Who Approves | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Routine operating expenses (within budget) | Office Administrator / Ministry staff | Treasurer review | Classified against approved budget lines |
| Unbudgeted expenses below threshold | Office Administrator | Treasurer + Finance Committee Chair | Threshold to be defined by Finance Committee |
| Unbudgeted expenses above threshold | Any | Full Finance Committee | Requires meeting or email vote per committee policy |
| Capital expenditures | Trustees | Church Council | Trustees recommend; Finance Committee assesses funding; Council approves |
| Staff compensation changes | SPRC | Church Council | SPRC recommends; Council approves per UMC polity |
| Memorial fund expenditures | Memorial Committee | Finance Committee | Memorial Committee recommends use; Finance Committee approves |
| Restricted fund expenditures | Relevant committee | Finance Committee | Must comply with donor intent and fund terms |
| Endowment earnings distribution | Finance Committee | Church Council / Board | Board appropriation required per endowment terms |
| Annual budget | Finance Committee | Church Council | Finance Committee prepares; Council approves |

## 4. Application Access — Preliminary

> Informed by Roles-and-Personas. This section captures which roles need
> what level of access to the expense coordination application.

| Role | Access Level | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| **Treasurer** | Full access — all functions | Owns financial accuracy and reporting |
| **Office Administrator** | Expense entry and status tracking | Primary operational user — enters and classifies expenses |
| **Finance Committee Chair** | Read-only — reports and summaries | Needs financial visibility without transaction entry |
| **Pastor** | Read-only — high-level summary only | Strategic visibility; not involved in transaction detail |
| **Finance Committee Members** | Read-only — reports | Periodic review for oversight purposes |
| **All others** | No access | Expenses submitted through Office Administrator |
